About Me

I’m a very goal-oriented designer. To me, the ultimate goal is to make someone’s life better through design. I have to give credit to my mother for instilling a passion to help others in me at an early age. Through this passion I have managed to aid practitioners in medical fields as well as help people who have disabilities through both design and volunteering.

I’m always looking for new ways to better myself and I always aim to make my designs so that they can be enjoyed by all. The fields of UX and UI work incredibly well together, but I hope to use my knowledge of accessibility to take the designs in these areas to new levels.

To learn more about my education and professional skills, review my resume.
